Pediatric Interventional Radiology with Surgical Correlations is a welcomed and much-needed medical text given the significant advances that have occurred in pediatric interventional radiology and surgery over the past decade. Equally important as the radiology-based information is the correlation with surgical practice and technique as an understanding of surgical practice and the perspective of the pediatric surgeon are critical to the performance of high-quality, clinically-relevant pediatric interventions.

Written by experts in pediatric Interventional radiology and pediatric surgery.
